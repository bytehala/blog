Emulating on Windows

If you noticed on the first screenshot, it's a Windows 7 screenshot. I emulated it on Bluestacks, as discussed on this AndEngine thread. (I can't use my phone because it's my portable hotspot, and there's problems like draining faster than USB charging, overheat, lag, etc.)

To launch your app from eclipse to the Bluestacks emulator, you have to type:

adb connect localhost:5555

After which, you just launch your app from eclipse as usual. Cool huh?
